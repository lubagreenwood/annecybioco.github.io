About Annecy Bio

Quietly building the next inflection point in aesthetics

Annecy Bio is a company developing a transformative technology and a first-in-class platform at the intersection of materials science and aesthetic medicine. Our approach represents a fundamental rethinking of how the body can be sculpted — with a mechanism that is elegant, precise, and entirely unlike anything currently on the market.

The platform

Precision fat loss and skin tightening — reimagined from first principles

Current body contouring forces a trade-off: invasive procedures with real results, or non-invasive options that under-deliver. We built a platform to eliminate that compromise entirely.

01

Novel mechanism of action

A biologically elegant approach that works with the body's own processes — selectively targeting fat while stimulating tissue renewal. No pharmacologic agent. No thermal injury.

02

Precision at the point of care

Clinician-controlled dosing and anatomical targeting that adapts to each patient. Sub-five-minute treatments. No capital equipment required.

03

Fat reduction and skin tightening are not two separate treatments — they are the natural, simultaneous outcome of a single platform mechanism.

The moment

Why the market is ready for a new approach to body contouring

The global aesthetic medicine market exceeds $100 billion, with body contouring and fat reduction among the fastest-growing segments. The rise of GLP-1 therapies is creating an entirely new patient population — tens of millions who have lost weight and now seek solutions for residual fat deposits and skin laxity. Meanwhile, existing non-surgical body contouring options remain limited by weak efficacy, high equipment costs, and patient dissatisfaction. Annecy Bio's platform is designed to meet this moment — a new category of fat loss and skin tightening technology built for the patients, clinicians, and economics of the next decade.
